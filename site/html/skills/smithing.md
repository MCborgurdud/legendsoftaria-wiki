# ![Smithing](/assets/images/skills/smithing.png) Smithing

**Smithing** is a production skill that allows players to smelt ores into bars at a furnace and forge those bars into weapons and armor at an anvil.

## Getting Started

Smithing has two components:
1. **Smelting**: Use a furnace to convert ores into metal bars
2. **Forging**: Use an anvil to craft bars into equipment

## Smelting

Use a furnace to smelt ores into bars. Each bar type requires a minimum Smithing level.

## Training Tips

1. **Levels 1-10**: Smelt [![Bronze Bar](/assets/images/items/bronze-bar.png) Bronze Bars](/items/bronze-bar.html) and smith Bronze equipment. Focus on swords first as they only need 1 bar.
2. **Levels 10-20**: Switch to [![Iron Bar](/assets/images/items/iron-bar.png) Iron](/items/iron-bar.html) smelting and smithing. The XP doubles compared to Bronze.
3. **Levels 20-30**: Work with [![Mithril Bar](/assets/images/items/mithril-bar.png) Mithril](/items/mithril-bar.html) for better XP rates.
4. **Levels 30+**: [![Adamant Bar](/assets/images/items/adamant-bar.png) Adamant](/items/adamant-bar.html) provides the best XP but requires significant material investment.

## Time Efficiency

- **Smelting**: 3 seconds per bar
- **Smithing**: 5 seconds per item

All smithing operations can be queued - select the item to craft, choose a quantity, and your character will continue until materials run out or you cancel.
