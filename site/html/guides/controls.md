# Controls

This page covers the controls for Legends of Taria.

## Mobile Controls

### Movement

Use the **virtual joystick** on the left side of the screen to move your character in any direction.

### Hotbar

Tap any **hotbar slot** at the bottom of the screen to select that item. The selected item will be used for your next interaction.

### Interaction

When you're near an interactable object (NPCs, rocks, fishing spots, enemies, etc.), an **interact button** will appear on screen near the object. Tap the button to interact with it.

- Standing near an NPC shows a talk button
- Standing near a rock with a pickaxe selected shows a mine button
- Standing near an enemy shows an attack button
- Standing near a fishing spot with a rod selected shows a fish button

## Desktop Controls

### Movement

| Key | Action |
|-----|--------|
| W | Move up |
| A | Move left |
| S | Move down |
| D | Move right |

You can also click to move, just like on mobile.

### Hotbar

The hotbar contains your most important items like weapons, tools, and food. Use number keys to quickly select items:

| Key | Hotbar Slot |
|-----|-------------|
| 1-9 | Select corresponding slot |

You can also click on hotbar slots to select them.

### Interaction

Desktop uses the same interaction system as mobile. When you're near an interactable object, an **interact button** appears on screen. Click the button or press the **spacebar** to interact.

- Standing near an NPC shows a talk button
- Standing near a rock with a pickaxe selected shows a mine button
- Standing near an enemy shows an attack button
- Standing near a fishing spot with a rod selected shows a fish button

### Chat

| Key | Action |
|-----|--------|
| Enter | Open chat / Send message |

Type your message and press Enter to send.

## Tips

1. **Hotbar is key**: Keep your weapon, pickaxe, fishing rod, and food in your hotbar for quick access
2. **Walk to interact**: You need to be close to objects and NPCs to interact with them
3. **Tap and hold**: On mobile, tap and hold to continuously move in a direction
